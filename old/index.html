Controls

  • Alphanumeric - type characters!
  • Enter - publish your work for money!
  • Tab - buy bananas!
  • Up/Down Arrows - choose a banana for feeding!
  • Shift + Tab - feed monkeys with the selected banana!
  • Shift + Right Arrow - move selected banana into the fridge!
  • Shift + Left Arrow - remove selected banana from the fridge!

Feeding Priority:

  1. Hangry monkeys - They're angry! They'll fight harder for bananas and will push to get to the front of the pack at feeding time.
  2. Hungry monkeys - They're relatively patient, but a banana would be nice.
  3. Angry monkeys - An extra banana treat will sate an angry monkey's frustration.
  4. Monkeys for hire - After monkey wellness has been completely tended to, more monkeys can be hired.

Price Differences: Organic vs. Non-Organic
Average price of non-organic bananas: $0.57 per lb.
Average price of organic bananas: $0.89 per lb.

THEREFORE:
Average price of 1 medium non-organic banana = $0.57 / 3 = $0.19
Average price of 1 medium organic banana = $0.89 / 3 = ~$0.30
